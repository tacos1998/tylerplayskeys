Welcome! I’m Tyler. I’m probably one of the few people you know who can change keys from C major to C++.

This is the part where I’m supposed to tell you about all my specializations. Things like full-stack development, front-end design, and music production and performance.

But the bigger thing behind all of those is creativity. And I can’t really say that I specialize in creativity, any more than I specialize in wanting salami sandwiches. Because that’s what creativity is for me: an appetite.

It’s built into me. It moves me. It works its way into everything I do. It’s a driving force that compels me to write songs, and programs, and sometimes both; it’s why I started an internship working on medical software, and found myself developing a procedurally-generated audio soundtrack for the media display in the lobby as a side project. It’s also why, almost every day that summer, I performed at Kansas City’s Union Station after work and ended up as an apprentice at a piano bar.

So, if you’re hungry for great software, great performances, or salami sandwiches, let’s get in touch.
