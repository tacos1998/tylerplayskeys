Jambr

Sponsor's Choice Award, HackISU 2016

Iowa State University in Ames, IA

Jambr is a Rails app that uses the Spotify API to generate context-specific playlists based on factors like the time of day, local weather, and the user's current mood.

I developed Jambr in collaboration with Jeremie Amano and Katherine Durant for HackISU 2016, an MLH-sanctioned hackathon at Iowa State University. Jambr won a Sponsor's Choice award.

For more info and a demo video, click or tap here to view the submission page on Devpost.

This was our first hackathon together, and we enjoyed the experience so much that we decided that it wouldn't be our last. The three of us continued to travel to hackathons, about once evey semester. After two years of competing, we won 2nd Place at TigerHacks 2018 for Censr, a profanity censor for live streams.
