Pregnancy Resource Center of Navarre Website

prcofnavarre.com

I started off helping with clerical tasks, but when the Executive Director found out that I had a background in technology, she had me take over the organization’s website.

The existing site was completely broken on mobile devices - text bursting out of containers, navbar links spilling all over each other, assets that didn’t resize for smaller viewports. The structure of the site was cluttered, and I noticed a lot of typos.

I made the site responsive, cleaned up the language, discussed the site structure with the staff to create a new structure that allowed clients to find the information they needed more effectively, and maintained an ongoing cycle of receiving feature requests from the staff, implementing them, and tweaking them based off of staff and client feedback.

I was also asked to research whether there were any digital marketing resources that were available to us. I set up our organization with Google for Nonprofits, and I created, launched, and maintained our first Google Ad campaign, which significantly increased website engagement and calls to the Center.
